# Butterflies of San Diego County

A visual identification guide to the butterflies of San Diego County, California.

**[View the guide](https://chetanddesai.github.io/sd-lepidoptera/)**

## About

This site provides identification descriptions, field marks, and photographs for every butterfly species known to breed in or regularly visit San Diego County. The checklist covers ~150 species across 6 families.

Species images are dynamically sourced from [iNaturalist](https://www.inaturalist.org) community observations (Creative Commons licensed) and cached locally for fast subsequent loads.

## Source

Species checklist sourced from the [Checklist of Butterflies of San Diego County](https://www.sdnhm.org/science/invertebrate-zoology/projects/checklist-of-butterflies-of-san-diego-county/) by the **San Diego Natural History Museum**, prepared by Nature Festivals of San Diego County with professional review by David Faulkner (SDNHM) and Dr. John Brown (Smithsonian Institute).

## Features

- Complete species checklist with field identification descriptions
- High-quality photographs from iNaturalist (lazy-loaded with local caching)
- Search by common or scientific name
- Filter by protected or rare status
- Responsive design for field use on mobile devices
- Family and subfamily navigation
- Wingspan, flight period, and habitat information for each species

## Tech

Static HTML/CSS/JS — no build step required. Designed for GitHub Pages deployment.
